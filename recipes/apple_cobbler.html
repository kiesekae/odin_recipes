Apple Cobbler

Apple Cobbler is a yummy desert. It is one of my favorites to have after a full meal.

Apple Cobbler might look like a complicated desert to make but youll see in a moment its not as hard as you think! It only takes a few ingredients. Some of which, you may already have in the kitchen! Oh! Did I mention you can make it in under an hour?!

Ingredients

  • 6 large Granny Smith apples - peeled, cored and sliced
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • Â¼ cup water

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Combine 3 tablespoons sugar and cinnamon in a bowl.
  2. Layer apples in an ungreased 9x13-inch baking pan, sprinkling each layer with cinnamon-sugar.
  3. Combine flour, 1 cup sugar, egg, and baking powder with a fork. Sprinkle over apples. Lightly sprinkle water over top.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until topping is golden brown, about 30 minutes.
